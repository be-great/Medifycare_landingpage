About Us

Welcome to Medifycare

The inspiration behind Medifycare came from a personal connection. One of our team members, Esra, had a close friend who envisioned a platform that would offer professional consulting services tailored to healthcare professionals. That spark of an idea motivated us to bring Medifycare to life. We wanted to create a reliable, AI-powered platform for healthcare consulting, bridging the gap between professionals and experts with seamless functionality.

This project has been a journey that began with a desire to build something impactful. Over the course of several months, we’ve developed and refined Medifycare as part of our portfolio project at Holberton School. We’ve combined our skills, driven by passion, to offer a solution that improves the healthcare consulting experience.
